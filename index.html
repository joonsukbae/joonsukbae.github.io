Joonsuk Bae

Experimental high-energy nuclear physics. I lead the first charged-particle jet measurements on Run 3 pp data with ALICE at the LHC, and work on Pb–scintillator-fiber calorimetry for the Electron–Ion Collider. Drawn to how non-perturbative QCD and initial-state effects shape jets in small systems.

Now

What I’m working on

ALICE Run 3
Lead analyzer of the first charged-particle jet cross-section measurement on Run 3 pp data at √s = 13.6 TeV, from detector-level QA through unfolding to the full systematic-uncertainty programme.
ePIC BIC
Beam-test analysis and JANA2 reconstruction studies of the Pb/SciFi calorimeter prototype for the Electron–Ion Collider; sampling-fraction, energy-resolution and linearity at CERN T10 and KEK AF–AR.
Jet substructure
Observables sensitive to hadronization and quenching-like signatures in high-multiplicity pp and light-ion systems.
